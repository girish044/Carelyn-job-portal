💡 Quick Career Tips

✅ Keep your resume to 1 page

Recruiters spend only 6-7 seconds on a resume. Keep it crisp, clean and relevant.

🔗 Optimize your LinkedIn profile

Add a professional photo, headline and skills. 87% of recruiters use LinkedIn.

🎯 Apply to jobs within 24 hours

Early applicants are 4x more likely to get an interview. Don't wait!

📝 Customize resume per job

Match keywords from the job description to pass ATS screening filters.

🤝 Network actively

80% of jobs are filled through networking. Connect with people in your field.

💪 Practice DSA daily

Solve at least 1-2 problems daily on LeetCode or HackerRank to stay sharp.
